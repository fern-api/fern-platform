Once you have [imported](importing-data-overview.mdx) your customers' financial data into Layer, you can start offering them Layer's full suite of accounting features within your platform. This includes:
* [Transaction Categorization](transaction-categorization.mdx) - Categorize customer bank transactions into revenue/expense categories and automatically reconcile bank transactions with revenue, payroll, and bill data. This ensures customers' financial data is properly recorded in their general ledger. 
* [Accounting Reports](reporting.mdx) - Surface real-time financial reports such as the profit and loss statement, balance sheet, or AR/AP aging summary.
